The Legend of Kaldi

Around 850 AD, a goat herder named Kaldi noticed his goats dancing with energy after eating red berries from a bush in the Kaffa highlands.

Spiritual Roots

Monks used the berries to stay awake during long night prayers, discovering the power of the "Buna" bean for the first time.

Traditional Ceremony

Coffee is not just a drink; it is a ritual of peace and community. The three rounds—Abol, Tona, and Baraka—bless the house.

The Birthplace of Arabica

Ethiopia is the only place on Earth where coffee grows wild. Thousands of varieties exist in the rainforests of the Southwest.

Ethiopia to the World

From Harar to Sidamo and Yirgacheffe, Ethiopia’s "Green Gold" fuels the global coffee market with the highest quality beans.
