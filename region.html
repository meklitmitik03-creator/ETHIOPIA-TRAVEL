Oromia

Heart of Ethiopia. Home to the Bale Mountains, coffee forests, and the Gadaa system.

Amhara

The Land of Kings. Explore the castles of Gonder and the source of the Blue Nile.

Tigray

Cradle of Civilization. Visit the ancient obelisks of Aksum and sky-high cliff churches.

Somali

The Eastern Gateway. Famous for its livestock trade, nomadic heritage, and vast plains.

Afar

The Danakil Depression. Visit the Erta Ale volcano and the hottest inhabited place on Earth.

Sidama

Land of Coffee and Fichee-Chambalaalla. Famous for lush green landscapes and the city of Hawassa.

Harari

The Walled City. Step into the ancient Jugol, the fourth holiest city in Islam.

Dire Dawa

The Railway City. A unique blend of modern planning and old-world charm.

South Ethiopia

Home to diverse ethnic groups including the Wolayta and Gamo, famous for rich cultural heritage.

Gambela

The Lowlands. Famous for the Baro River and amazing migratory wildlife.

Benishangul

Home of the Grand Ethiopian Renaissance Dam (GERD) and rich mineral deposits.

Central Ethiopia

The cultural heartland, featuring Gurage and Silte heritages and beautiful highlands.
