Kingdom of D'mt

The earliest known kingdom (c. 980 BC), centered at Yeha. A civilization of advanced architecture and iron-working.

Aksumite Empire

One of the four great powers of the ancient world. Famous for its towering obelisks and being the first to adopt Christianity.

Zagwe Dynasty

The era of King Lalibela. A golden age of religious art and the creation of the 11 rock-hewn churches.

Solomonic Dynasty

Tracing their lineage to King Solomon and the Queen of Sheba, this dynasty ruled until the 20th century.

Adal Sultanate

A powerful multi-ethnic Muslim state centered in Zeila and Harar, famous for its trade and military strength.

Kingdom of Kafa

The birthplace of Coffee. A sovereign kingdom with deep forests and complex administrative systems.
